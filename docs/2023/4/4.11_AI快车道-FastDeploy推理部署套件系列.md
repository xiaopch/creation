## AI快车道-FastDeploy推理部署套件系列



**⚡️FastDeploy**是一款**全场景**、**易用灵活**、**极致高效**的AI推理部署工具， 支持**云边端**部署。提供超过 🔥160+ **Text**，**Vision**， **Speech**和**跨模态**模型📦**开箱即用**的部署体验，并实现🔚**端到端**的推理性能优化。包括 [物体检测](https://gitee.com/paddlepaddle/FastDeploy/blob/develop/examples/vision/detection)、[字符识别（OCR）](https://gitee.com/paddlepaddle/FastDeploy/blob/develop/examples/vision/ocr)、[人脸](https://gitee.com/paddlepaddle/FastDeploy/blob/develop/examples/vision/facedet)、[人像扣图](https://gitee.com/paddlepaddle/FastDeploy/blob/develop/examples/vision/matting)、[多目标跟踪系统](https://gitee.com/paddlepaddle/FastDeploy/blob/develop/examples/vision/tracking/pptracking)、[NLP](https://gitee.com/paddlepaddle/FastDeploy/blob/develop/examples/text)、[Stable Diffusion文图生成](https://gitee.com/paddlepaddle/FastDeploy/blob/develop/examples/multimodal/stable_diffusion)、[TTS](https://gitee.com/paddlepaddle/FastDeploy/blob/develop/examples/audio/pp-tts) 等几十种任务场景，满足开发者**多场景、多硬件、多平台**的产业部署需求。
